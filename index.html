HTML5 Canvas Demos

Some experiences with HTML5 canvas, graphics and libraries.

Most of the examples come from old 80's and 90's books, mostly in BASIC (8-bit) and I will be porting or retrofitting them to work with modern tools such as HTML5 canvas, Python and so on.

Remembering the old type-in books and magazines, it always amazed me how some lines of BASIC code could generate awesome graphics on the screen.

A couple of decades later I am looking back at them and trying to understand how they work and what mathematical formulas are behind it.
